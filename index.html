My Journey

It all started when I was 12 and wanted to learn how to record and edit videos and design thumbnails for YouTube. That’s when I began my self-taught journey, continuing to grow my skills through college.

Since then, I’ve specialized in graphic design, web design, and sound production — combining creativity with technical skill to build engaging digital experiences.
